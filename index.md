Privacy Policy — MinoTimer

Effective date: 10th Feb 2026

MinoTimer is designed to help you create calm, structured routines. Your privacy matters to us.

Information we collect

MinoTimer does not require you to create an account.
The app stores your timers and routines locally on your device. We do not collect or store the content of your timers on our servers.
We may collect limited technical information such as crash reports or performance data to help improve app stability.

Subscriptions

MinoTimer offers optional subscriptions. Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple App Store and Google Play using RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Third-party services

MinoTimer uses the following third-party services:

Apple App Store / Google Play (payments)

RevenueCat (subscription management)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

User control

You can delete the app at any time to remove locally stored data from your device. Subscriptions can be managed or cancelled through your App Store or Google Play account settings.

Children’s privacy

MinoTimer is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
